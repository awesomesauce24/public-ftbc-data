== Info ==
[[Desert]] is a sandy area with lots of cacti, a few desert houses near the back of the area, and a pyramid in the center. there are also other things like the giant cactus that sends you to the [[Barren Desert]] or [[Rusty Coin]]'s lemonade stand.
