== How to enter ==

First, go to where [[Blue Shiny Coiny]] is, at Spawn.

Next, go to the left corner.  

Finally, jump down and touch the miscolored area.

== Info ==
Have you ever wondered where the unstable objects went, and why the main realm only really has normal looking objects? Welcome to the inverted realm, an upside down Rusty decayed version of the main realm where all these objects live, pretty much everything has a weird quark about them.
