== How to enter ==
{{Location|title=Yoyleland|image=[[File:Yoyleland.png]]}}
Touch a purple bush in [[Goiky]], near [[Rubber Spatula]]

== Info ==
[[Yoyleland]] is a large city filled with tall buildings, strange terrain, and a massive purple hill.  
In addition to this, There is a canal called the Goiky Canal, containing several objects you can collect.
