== Info ==
[[Factory]] Is a giant Factory with an outside section and an inside section. On the outside, you will find some lamp posts and [[Match]], who crashed The Freesmart Supervan right into the factory. On the roof you will find many pipes, which contain can be scaled up using various methods. On the inside of the factory, you will find a few conveyors and many shelves, which all have many boxes on them. This area is bordered by [[Number Playground]] and [[Tropical Islands]].
