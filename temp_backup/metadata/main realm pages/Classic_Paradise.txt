== How To Enter ==
* Go to '''[[Classic Roblox]]'''
* Find the '''white button''' located on the tall tower.
* Press it — this spawns '''5 white orbs''' around the area.
* Collect all 5 orbs:
** On top of the tower
** Underneath the tower
** In front of the castle
** Near '''[[Teardrop]]'''
** In front of the wooden house door

* After grabbing all 5, you can teleport into Classic Paradise.

== Info ==

'''Classic Paradise''' is a bright and vibrant realm, with three areas: '''Cave, Greenhouse, and Village (Spawn)'''. This realm contains about '''50(+2) objects''' in total!
