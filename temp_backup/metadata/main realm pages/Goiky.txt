==Info==
'''Goiky''' is the main area of [[Find the BFB Characters|FTBC]]. In [[goiky]], you will find houses towards the north and trees towards the east. Goiky connects to [[Candyland]] and [[Snowy]]. in the fall, goikys foliage becomes orange and in the winter, snow, candycanes, and presents appear.
