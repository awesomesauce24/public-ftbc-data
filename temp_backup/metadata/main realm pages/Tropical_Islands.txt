==Info==
[[Tropical Islands]] is one of the many areas in the [[Main Realm]]. This area borders 4 areas, being [[Classic Roblox]],[[Factory]], [[Hotel]], and [[Number Playground]]. This area consists of many sandy islands with sun chairs, umbrellas, and palm trees. Far away, there is another big island with a box pushing puzzle for [[Evil Bubble]].
