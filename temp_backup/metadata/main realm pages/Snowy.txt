== Info ==
[[Snowy]] is a Cold area covered in snow and ice. This biome borders [[Goiky]], [[Jungle]], and [[City]]. This area has a few cabins across it which have some objects inside. There is also a ball pushing puzzle for [[Snowball]] east of the biome.
