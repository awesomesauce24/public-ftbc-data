== How to enter ==
Climb [[Bell]]'s string at [[Goiky]]. Then jump to the clouds. Go straight from [[Puffball]].

== Info ==
Timber Peaks is a autumn mountain high above the clouds. Similar to [[Classic Paradise]], almost the entire place is studded. To get to the top of the mountain, there will be an obby near spawn which will lead you to the top.
