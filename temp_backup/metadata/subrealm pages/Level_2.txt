== How to Enter ==

Find an exit door in [[Level 1]].

== Info ==
[[Level 2]] (also known as '''Pipe Dreams''') is a maze-like industrial area filled with long hallways, exposed piping, and constant ambient noise.  
It is considered more hostile than [[Level 1]] and shares a similar unsettling atmosphere to the [[Evil Forest]].
