">INFORMATION</span> ==

'''Level 0''' is the first Backrooms-style sub-realm encountered in the game.  
It appears as a maze of yellowed rooms with mossy carpeting, flickering fluorescent lights, and a constant loud buzzing hum.

To exit the level, players must navigate until they locate a surveillance room containing cameras.  
The exit door will always be located within this room.
