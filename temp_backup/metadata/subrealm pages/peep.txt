==Obtaining==
Find an egg in every single realm, then head to [[Classic Roblox]] and find a small black teleporter in the pillars of the crossroads tower.

==== List of Eggs ====



Main Realm - Next to a bush at [[Hotel]].

Yoyleland - On top of the building next to the matchstick factory.

Inverted  - On a tree near the hotel.

Backrooms - In [[Level 0]].

Classic Paradise - Near the blue torch in the cave.

Cherry Grove - Go to the invisible platforms leading to [[Sakura Serenity]], turn behind the wall and hug it to enter the hole. 

Sakura Serenity - Near Bug Spray on the water.

Barren Desert - In the rusty building near the town.

Evil Forest - In Gelatin's steakhouse.

Midnight Rooftops - Near Starie.

Yoylefactory - In DUMMY3’s room.

Magma Canyon - Behind the mountain that [[Mr. Feet]] is on. Use the crane to get to the egg.

Timber Peaks - Halfway through [[Stump's]] obby.

Polluted Marshlands - In some bushes near [[China]].

Frozen World - halfway through Freeze Juice’s obby on the giant platform.
