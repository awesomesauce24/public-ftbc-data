==Trivia==
*Hypostasis is one of the only 3 objects to have their sprite floating, the others being [[Firey]] with his Halloween costume and [[Asteroid]].
**Additionally, they have a particle effect. Making them one of the 3 objects with particle effects, with the others being [[Sphere of Benevolence]] and [[Great Rhombicosidodecahedron|The Great Rhombicosidodecahedron]].
*Hypostasis has a hidden version of their hint, which can be unlocked by entering a hidden /e command.
**The code to unlock it is mentioned in [[Great Rhombicosidodecahedron]]'s page. (go check it, it's the 2nd one)
***This is also true for it's hint, which is unlocked with the same /e command.
