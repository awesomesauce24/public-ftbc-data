== Info ==
Level 3 is a giant passage of tunnels, similar to [[Level 2]]. The level is dim, having no main light sources, but not almost pitch black like [[Level 2]].


== How to Enter ==
Find the exit door in [[Level 2]] and find the exit, A map can be found on its page.
