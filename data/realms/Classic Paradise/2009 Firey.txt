== Character ==
[[Firey]]'s old design from Firey's Candy Bar Adventure.

== Obtaining ==
2009 Firey requires you to find 6 different Roblox gears around the map of [[Classic Paradise]], you will know you got them if your given a badge.

* The Rocket is on the roof of the Greenhouse.
* The Superball is behind the yellow [[Infinity]] obby.
* The Bomb is on the island with [[Fruit Bowl]] behind the Greenhouse.
* The Trowel is on the side of the island with the giant tree.
* The Slingshot is in the 1st floor of the cave in the path infront of the one leading to [[Emerald]].
* The Sword is on the 2nd floor of the cave where you take a right and then take a left at the start of the floor.

After getting all 6 gears, head to [[Starter Place]] and find 2009 Firey in one of the corners of the map.
