== Info ==
'''Starter Place''' is uhhh... filled with old roblox stuff.<br>
'''Starter place''' is also where you get to [[Classic Insanity]] and the code for [[Replicated Storage]]. You also get 2009 Firey here.

== How to Enter ==
Start in [[Classic Paradise]], then go to the island with the big tree. If you look behind the island, there's a green part in between the rocks. Touch it and you'll be teleporter to '''Starter Place'''.
