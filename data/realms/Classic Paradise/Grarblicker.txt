==Character==
[[Grarblicker]] is a Terrifying object located in [[Classic Paradise]]. They are a Grarblicker with a neutral Expression. To get them, you must find five buttons around [[Classic Paradise]].
==Obtaining==
Start in [[Classic Paradise]], then click these buttons:
* On one of the crates inside the small cave
* Under one of the leaves of the palm tree in front of the last obby for Infinity
* In one of the benches in the island with the big tree
* Behind the house near the small cave
* Under the bridge to the first obby for Infinity
