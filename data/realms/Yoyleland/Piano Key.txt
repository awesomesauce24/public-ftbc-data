== Info ==
It is a piano key with two faces and 3 total arms, one for the black part and one for the white part. the white part seems to have a frown while the black part seems to have a more surprised expression.

== Obtaining ==
In [[Yoyleland]], Go to where [[Kennel]] is and look behind the desk in the building they are in, Piano Key should be behind it.
