==Info==
A flying piece of carrot cake with no limbs or face.

==Obtaining==
In Yoyleland, go to where [[Choo Choo The Train]] is and hop onto the roof of the building next to it, from that building you will be able to then jump down and collect Carrot Cake who is hovering over a road.
