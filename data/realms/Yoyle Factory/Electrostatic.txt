A red Positive Charge and a blue Negative Charge.

== Obtaining ==
In the section of [[Yoyle Factory/Meltdown|Meltdown]] near [[Delicious Fruit]], Direct the bullets from the turret to hit the nearby pillar in the acid 3 times. once you do this, Electrostatic will appear where the pillar was.
