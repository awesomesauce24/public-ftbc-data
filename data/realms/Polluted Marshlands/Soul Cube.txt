== Character ==
The object is a version of [[Ice Cube]] with inverted colors.

== Obtaining ==
On the starting island of [[Polluted Marshlands]], there will be a platform that sometimes appears on the shore. Get on this platform and ride towards the island left of spawn. Soul Cube will be on this island.

== Trivia ==

* Soul Cube was actually requested to be added to the game by alexlion0511 himself, recommender of [[Marker]] and creator of [https://www.roblox.com/games/7896264844/Find-the-Markers Find the Markers].
** Yes, he did in fact like the game.
