== Character ==
'''Ellipsis''' is three circles, two eyes and the other a body, with hands and legs. They appear tan in color.

== Obtaining ==
Go to the building that [[Cardboard]] is at, then look below the wooden logs at the back of it. 

== Notes ==
The part of the mountain below '''Ellipsis''' is missing collision in certain parts, causing you to fall through.
