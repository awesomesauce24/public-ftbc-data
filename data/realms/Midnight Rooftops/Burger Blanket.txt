== Info ==
A layered [[Burger]], with the shape of blankets.

== Obtaining ==
Find a way to climb to the top of the grass roof (where you can get [[Not Canon]] and [[Line Coin]]) After that, turn on Click to Move, via Camera Settings. Click on Burger Blanket, and it should automatically take you there.
