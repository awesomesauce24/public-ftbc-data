== Info ==
A gray playing card with a heart symbol and a simple face.

== Obtaining ==
Find all 9 hidden text pieces around the map.

{| class="wikitable mw-collapsible mw-collapsed"

1. Near [[Shrimp Tempura]] and [[Robot Puppet]], underneath the platform.<br>
2. Start at the tallest carrot. Get on top, then drop to the right of [[refsbcj]].<br>
3. On the stack of boxes near [[Mr. Feet]].<br>
4. Drop down near [[Gas Canister]].<br>
5. Once you reach the [[Erlenmeyer]] concrete platform, the text is on the bottom-right pillar. Drop to the floor below.<br>
6. Below the first step on the wooden bridge, behind [[Spongy's mother]].<br>
7. Near the lavafall at spawn. Look on the wall near the lava.<br>
8. On the second stack of crates used to reach the tallest carrot, behind the middle box.<br>
9. Near [[Shoe]]. Check the corners.
