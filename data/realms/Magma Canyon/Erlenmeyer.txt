== Info ==
A light teal Erlenmeyer flask with a wide base, narrow top, and a happy face.

== Obtaining ==
Start near [[Mr. Feet]], and get to the crane behind the map. Near the lava is an opaque yellow platform. Drop down and use click-to-move to reach the concrete area that the platform is facing.
