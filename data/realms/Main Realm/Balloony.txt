== Character ==
A green balloon with a string.

== Obtaining ==
Teleport to [[City]], then go to the Headquarters building and climb all the way to the top.  
From the roof, look toward the [[Fries]] restaurant: Balloony is sitting on top of it.  
Turn on sprint and jump over to reach him.

== Info ==
Balloony is a green balloon with a small knot at the bottom and a thin string hanging downward.
