== Character ==
Fire Spatula appears as a glowing spatula engulfed in flames with a cheerful face.

== Obtaining ==
Start in City and grab the shovel inside the dumpster near [[Barbecue Sauce]]. Then travel to Tropical Islands and look for the red X on the ground. Touch it to dig and reveal Fire Spatula buried below.
