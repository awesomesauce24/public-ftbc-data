== Character ==
It's in the name. A handheld, portable music player.

== Obtaining ==
Go to [[City]], then enter the Library.  

Click the camera on the desk, look behind [[Yellow Face]], and click on [[Purple Face]].  

This teleports you to [[Purple Face Mouth]]; complete the obby to reach Portable Music Player.
