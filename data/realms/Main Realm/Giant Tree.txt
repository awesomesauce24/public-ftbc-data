== Character ==
Giant Tree appears as a tall tree with a thick trunk and a leafy green canopy.

== Obtaining ==
Begin by following the steps for [[Titanium Knife]]. Once the ladder becomes available, climb up and enter the cannon. After being launched, walk forward to reach Giant Tree.
