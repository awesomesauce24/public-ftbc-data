== How to enter ==
Go to the sewers to where [[Immortality Thing]] is and then look towards the wall of the sewers.<br>
And on your right jump down and eventually you would land on an invisible path.<br>
At the end of the invisible path you should see a green tile and touch it.<br>
You will have to jump off the path for it.<br>
