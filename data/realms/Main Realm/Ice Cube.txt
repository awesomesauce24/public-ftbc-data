'''Ice Cube''' (often addressed as '''Icy''') is a female contestant that competed in ''[[Battle for Dream Island]], [[Battle for Dream Island Again]]'', ''[[IDFB]]'', ''[[Battle for BFDI]]'', and is currently competing in ''[[Battle for Dream Island: The Power of Two]]''. In the comic series, she placed third overall in [[Total Firey Island]], and was the runner up in [[Total Firey Switch]].

== Obtaining ==
Find a pile of snowball at [[Snowy]], then click on it. After that, throw it to the target then '''Ice Cube''' will rise from the ice behind you.

==Trivia==
*This used to be a Easy but on update 2.16.3, it changed to Hard.
