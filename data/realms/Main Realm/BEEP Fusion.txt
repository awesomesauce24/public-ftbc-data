==Character==
<b>Beep Fusion</b> is a Remorseless character which is a fusion of all characters from Beep Fusion.
==Obtaining==
Go to Level 4 of [[Backrooms|The Backrooms]] the dance clip outside. Find a glowing orb then touch it (you'll be teleported to [[stairwell]]). After that, just go down til you spot it.
