== Obtaining ==
In [[Snowy]], press the button that spawns a Snowball.  
Roll it along the path carefully and guide it to the end.  
Collect Snowball once it reaches the final platform.

== Info ==
Snowball is a bright white sphere with smooth shading and a clean outline.
