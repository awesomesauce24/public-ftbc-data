== Obtaining ==
In [[Number Playground]], Pin is lodged inside a tree.  
Simply walk up and collect her.

== Info ==
Pin is a tall purple pushpin with a rounded top and a pointed metal tip.  
She has oval black eyes and a small straight mouth, with thin black stick-like limbs.
