== Obtaining ==
Start in the City.  Find [[Camera]] and click the red square on it.  Yellow Face will appear after interacting with the square. [[Purple Face]] can also be found here.

== Info ==
Yellow Face is a bright yellow oval-shaped character with a thick black outline.  He has two black oval eyes and a wide, simple black smile.  His appearance is flat and stylized, resembling a cartoon face drawn on a smooth yellow surface.
