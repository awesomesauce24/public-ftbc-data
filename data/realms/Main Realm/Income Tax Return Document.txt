==Info==
Income Tax Return Document appears as a white tax form.

==Obtaining==
Go to Fries' Restaurant and press the emergency button behind the counter. Move to the right side and jump onto the sideways metal pole. Carefully walk across it to the other end, then go through the non-collidable wall to reach the document.
