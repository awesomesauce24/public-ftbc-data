== Character ==
Mr. Fish appears as a grey cartoon fish with a pink tie.
== Obtaining ==
From the Classic Roblox beach, ride the moving platform across the water. When it reaches the ships, jump onto the rightmost ship (might have to jump from the left ship). Mr. Fish is inside of the stove.
