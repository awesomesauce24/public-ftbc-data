== Character ==
Tri-Tri appears as three separate blue triangles with teal outlines, each showing a simple expression.

== Obtaining ==
Tri-Tri is found by clicking a blue nose on one of the snowmen in the Snowy area. The triangle pieces will appear once the correct snowman is interacted with.
