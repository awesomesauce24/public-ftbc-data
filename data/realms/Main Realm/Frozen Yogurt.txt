== Character ==
Frozen Yogurt appears as a cup filled with light pink frozen yogurt and a simple smiling face.

== Obtaining ==
Talk to [[Cake Pop]]. They hint at drawing a spiral. On the carpet, draw the spiral shape they describe, and Frozen Yogurt will appear once the pattern is completed.
