== Character ==
Hailairhous appears as a thick, teal book with a white page block and a friendly, simple face near the spine.

== Obtaining ==
Hailairhous is found underneath a tree, located behind the [[Bacon]] cabin in the Snowy area.  He is on the bottom side of the leaves, tucked in a corner.
