== Character ==
Vibe Check X appears as a large yellow “X” with exaggerated eyes and a raised hand ready to deliver a dramatic vibe check.

== Obtaining ==
Start near [[Gaty]] in the Snowy area, then go to the right-side cabin and look for the white gate beside it.

Vibe Check X is hidden directly behind the gate. simply hug the gate to collect him.
