== Info ==
Two is a green non-binary number “2”, who is also the host of "The Power of Two"

== Obtaining ==
Start in [[Goiky]].  
Go to the area near [[Bell]]; there is an invisible platform path you can walk on.  
Follow the path all the way to the end, then click the invisible box to obtain Two.
