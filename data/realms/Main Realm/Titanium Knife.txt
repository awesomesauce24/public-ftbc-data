== Character ==
Titanium Knife appears as a reflective iridescent knife with a gray handle.

== Obtaining ==
In the Desert, go to the upper left corner and look for a bush. A button is hidden on the ground beneath it. Press the button, then return to the Warehouse. Climb the ladder that appeared on the left side.
