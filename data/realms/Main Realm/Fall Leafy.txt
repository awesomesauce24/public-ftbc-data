==Character==
A fall version of [[Leafy]]. Clux's favorite character in the game most likely.
==Obtaining==
After getting to the roof of the Hotel, you can drop down to a small extension for the first and second floors. You can find a tree obby from there, completing it will bring you to Fall Leafy.
