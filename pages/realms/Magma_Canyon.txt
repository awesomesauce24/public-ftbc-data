== How to enter ==
{{Location
 |title=Magma Canyon
 |image=[[File:Magma Canyon.png]]
}}

Go to [[Barren Desert]] and reach [[Sierpinski]]’s obby.  
After completing the first wrap-around, look beneath the platform. There will be an image of Magma Canyon.  
Touch it to teleport into the realm.

== Info ==
[[Magma Canyon]] is a burning, carrot-filled lava world with large mounds of scorched terrain.  
Several structures from [[Midnight Rooftops]], including cranes, can also be seen partially sunken into the molten landscape.
