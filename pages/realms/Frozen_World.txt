== Info ==
[[Frozen World]] is a frozen wasteland of ice, there are chunks of ice around the map with characters from the main Realm encased in them, examples include [[Firey]], [[Boom Mic]], [[Dane]], and others. Among the icy wasteland you can find objects like [[Octahedron]] and [[Rosin]]. The emperor of frozen World is [[Freeze Juice]], Whos goal is to encase the whole FTBC universe in a thick sheet of ice. 

==How to Enter==
Start by dance clipping to this wall at [[Hotel|Hotel]]

[[File:Dcw.png|180x180px|the wall]]

then grab the key. After that, go to [[EXIT|EXIT]] then unlock the door in the back (the one that leads to [[Oodle]], Then touch this wall to get the blue key.

[[File:Touchthewall.png|180x180px|touch it]]

Then, go to [[City|City]] Library, click at Camera's lens, then click at [[Yellow Face]] to go to [[Purple Face Mouth]]. Once your here, go to the blue pod with the key and it will open revealing a button. Once you click this button, a new obby will appear in [[Snowy]], complete this obby and you will be teleported to [[Frozen World]].
[[File:FrozenWorldEntrance.png|180x180px|FrozenWorldEntrance]]
