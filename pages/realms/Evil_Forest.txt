== <span style="color:#ff3b3b; text-shadow:0 0 14px red;">Info</span> ==
[[Evil Forest]] is a corrupted realm inspired by the Evil Forest seen mainly in ''Battle for Dream Island Again''.  
The environment is perpetually dark, with abandoned structures buried beneath dense red fog.  
Distances feel distorted, and objects may appear closer or farther than they truly are.

It contains two known Sub-Realms: [[Evle Froets]] and [[Evefdpfroestost]].  
<span style="color:#ff5c5c; text-shadow:0 0 18px red;"><small>You are not alone.</small></span>

== <span style="color:#ff3b3b; text-shadow:0 0 14px red;">How to Enter</span> ==
{{Location|title=Evil Forest|image=[[File:EVIL.png]]}}

'''NEW METHOD:'''  
Start in [[Tropical Islands]].  
Travel to the area where [[Swiss Roll]] is located and use the nearby boat to reach an island containing safes.  
Take one of the pushboxes and move it outside the wire barrier, then push it toward the pier.  
Continue pushing â€” an invisible path extends beyond the pier.  
At the invisible wall, stand on the pushbox to teleport into Evil Forest.
