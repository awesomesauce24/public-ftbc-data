== Info ==
'''Barren Desert''' is a wide, open desert region filled with rolling dunes, scattered cactuses, a small abandoned city, three rusty desert temples, and several sand hills. There is also an obby that serves various purposes under the map.

== How to Enter ==
Touch the Giant Cactus in the [[Desert]] to enter the realm.
