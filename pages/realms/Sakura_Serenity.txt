== How to enter ==
{{Location|title=Sakura Serenity|image=[[File:Sakura Serenity.png]]}}
Go to [[Cherry Grove]]. Find a hidden path behind where you spawn, Then do some parkour to the top of the building.

== Info ==
[[Sakura Serenity]] looks alot like Japan and has some japanese buildings and cherry trees. [[Cherry Grove]] is only about a few minutes away from [[Sakura Serenity]] as you can see it from [[Sakura Serenity]].
