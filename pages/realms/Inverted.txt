{{Location
 |title=Inverted Realm
 |image=[[File:Inverted-realm-image.png|Inverted Realm]]

First, go to where [[Blue Shiny Coiny]] is, at Spawn.

Next, go to the left corner.  

Finally, jump down and touch the miscolored area.

== Info ==
[[Inverted]] is an upside down version of the [[Main Realm]], with most textures now rusted and decaying.
