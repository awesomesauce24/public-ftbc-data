== How to Enter ==

First, go to [[Liy]]'s room. Next, find a wall with can-collide disabled. Finally, enter the teleporter.

== Info ==
'''Midnight Rooftops''' takes place atop the fog-covered skyline of a large, shadowy city.  
The area is filled with tall buildings, cranes, scaffolding, and ongoing construction.
