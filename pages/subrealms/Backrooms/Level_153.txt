">Info</span> ==

'''Level 153''', known as '''Antique Workspace''', is an office-themed sub-realm inspired by its Backrooms counterpart.  
Sprint is disabled in this level.

Upon entry, players experience light screen static and an increased field of view (FOV). The environment consists of dimly lit office rooms, outdated furniture, flickering lights, and malfunctioning electronics. The longer one remains inside, the more disorienting the atmosphere becomes. 

----

== <span style="
	background:linear-gradient(180deg,linen,#F1A176);
	-webkit-background-clip:text;
	-webkit-text-fill-color:transparent;
	text-shadow:0 0 8px #F1A176;
	letter-spacing:2px;
">How to Enter</span> ==

From [[Level 4]], walk all the way to the right upon entering the level and locate a translucent door.  
Beyond it is an opaque door; to pass through, you must [[roblox-glitches:Dance_Clip|Dance Clip]] through it and descend a staircase leading to an exit door.

====== Note ======
There exists another door labeled “Exit” in [[Level 4]]. It does nothing.
