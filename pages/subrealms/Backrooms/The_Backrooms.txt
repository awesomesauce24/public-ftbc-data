== Info ==
[[Level 0]] looks like a yellow room.<br>
[[Level 1]] looks like a parking lot.<br>
[[Level 2]] looks like a very old abandoned building. There is a monster present.<br>
[[Level 3]] looks like a maze of rooms with pipes and cages.<br>
[[Level 4]] looks like a paradise office with many rooms.<br>
<small>[[Stairwell]] is a sub-realm of Level 4, inspired by SCP-087.</small><br>
[[Level 153]] looks like an abandoned office with furniture.<br>
[[RUN FOR YOUR LIFE]] looks like a hospital with planks, beds, and doors.<br>
[[The End]] looks like an abandoned library with shelves and a monitor.
<small style="color:#9a9a9a;"><br>
This index documents known objects recovered across the Backrooms.
</small>
