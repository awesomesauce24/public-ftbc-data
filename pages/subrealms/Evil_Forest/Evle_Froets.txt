== <span style="color:#ff3b3b; text-shadow:0 0 14px red;">Info</span> ==
'''Evle Froets''' is a distorted sub-realm of [[Evil Forest]], filled with thick red fog and unstable geometry.  
Depth perception is unreliable, and distances may subtly shift while moving.  
Some structures appear partially phased into the fog itself.

The realm is quieter than Evil Forest, yet feels more hostile.

<hr style="border:1px solid #330000;">

=== <span style="color:#ff3b3b; text-shadow:0 0 14px red;">How To Enter</span> ===
Start in [[Evil Forest]] and locate a white platform near [[Evil David]].  
The platform is invisible unless viewed from below.  
Perform three wrap-arounds, then climb the ladder at the end to reach Evle Froets.
